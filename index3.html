Workout Plan

6-Day Upper Body & Core Focus

Bench Press

3 sets x 8 reps

Lie on a flat bench, grip the bar slightly wider than shoulder-width. Lower the bar to your mid-chest and press it back up.

Bent-over Row

3 sets x 8 reps

Hinge at your hips with a flat back. Pull the barbell towards your lower chest, squeezing your back muscles.

Overhead Press

3 sets x 10 reps

Stand with the bar on your front shoulders. Press the bar overhead until your arms are fully extended.

Face Pulls

3 sets x 12 reps

Use a rope attachment on a cable machine. Pull the rope towards your face, keeping your elbows high and wide.

Plank

3 sets x 45 seconds

Hold a push-up position on your forearms. Keep your body in a straight line from head to heels.

Pull-ups

3 sets x 8 reps

Grip the bar with hands wider than shoulder-width. Pull your chest to the bar.

Dumbbell Chest Press

3 sets x 10 reps

Lie on a bench with a dumbbell in each hand. Press them up over your chest.

Seated Cable Row

3 sets x 10 reps

Sit at a cable row machine. Pull the handle to your midsection, squeezing your back.

Lateral Raises

3 sets x 12 reps

Stand with dumbbells at your sides. Raise them out to the sides until they're at shoulder height.

Hollow Hold

3 sets x 30 seconds

Lie on your back. Lift your shoulders and legs off the floor, keeping your lower back pressed down.

Incline DB Press

3 sets x 8 reps

Lie on an incline bench. Press dumbbells up from your upper chest.

TRX Rows

3 sets x 10 reps

Lean back holding TRX handles. Pull your chest towards the handles.

Hip Bridge

3 sets x 12 reps

Lie on your back with knees bent. Lift your hips towards the ceiling, squeezing your glutes.

Cable Face Pull

3 sets x 15 reps

Use a rope on a high pulley. Pull towards your face, externally rotating your shoulders.

Pallof Press

3 sets x 12 reps

Stand sideways to a cable machine. Hold the handle at your chest and press it straight out, resisting rotation.

Dips

3 sets x 8 reps

Use parallel bars. Lower your body until your shoulders are below your elbows, then press back up.

Single-arm Row

3 sets x 10 reps

Kneel on a bench with one knee and hand. Row a dumbbell up to your chest.

Seated Overhead Press

3 sets x 8 reps

Sit on a bench with back support. Press dumbbells or a barbell overhead.

Band Pull-Aparts

3 sets x 20 reps

Hold a resistance band with both hands. Pull the band apart by squeezing your shoulder blades.

Dead-bug

3 sets x 12 reps

Lie on your back with arms and knees in the air. Lower opposite arm and leg slowly, then return.

Chest Supported Row

3 sets x 8 reps

Lie face down on an incline bench. Row dumbbells up, squeezing your back.

Weighted Push-ups

3 sets x 12 reps

Perform a standard push-up with a weight plate on your back.

Inverted Rows

3 sets x 10 reps

Lie under a fixed bar (like in a Smith machine). Pull your chest up to the bar.

Rear-delt Fly

3 sets x 15 reps

Hinge at the hips with a flat back. Raise dumbbells out to your sides, squeezing your rear delts.

Side Plank

3 sets x 30s per side

Lie on your side, supported by one forearm. Keep your body in a straight line.

Machine Chest Press

3 sets x 8 reps

Sit at a chest press machine. Press the handles forward until your arms are extended.

Cable Row

3 sets x 10 reps

Use a seated cable row machine. Pull the handle towards your abdomen.

Farmer's Carry

3 sets x 40m

Hold a heavy dumbbell or kettlebell in each hand. Walk for the specified distance, keeping your core tight and posture upright.

Face Pulls

3 sets x 15 reps

Use a rope attachment on a cable machine. Pull the rope towards your face, keeping your elbows high and wide.

Hanging Knee Raises

3 sets x 12 reps

Hang from a pull-up bar. Raise your knees towards your chest without swinging.
